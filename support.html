Common Troubleshooting

  • App Performance & Stability: Make sure your iPhone is updated to a compatible iOS version and that you have downloaded the latest build of HabitAnchor directly from the App Store.
  • Premium Subscriptions Not Activating: If you purchased a subscription but features remain locked, go to the settings/paywall layout in HabitAnchor and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Data & Cloud Syncing: HabitAnchor uses secure cloud infrastructure to back up your routine data safely. If your data isn't syncing immediately across states, please verify that your device has an active internet connection and that you are logged into your account profile inside the app.
