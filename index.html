Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

HabitAnchor (“HabitAnchor”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we process it, and your legal rights regarding your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (if authenticated via Google or Apple Sign-In)
  • Firebase/Firestore authentication identifiers
  • Anonymized system ID (for unauthenticated local use)

Habit Tracking Data

  • Custom habit names, strategies, and descriptions
  • Daily and weekly milestone completion logs
  • Performance metrics, goals, and recurring notification settings

Health & Apple HealthKit Data (Optional)

  • Activity metrics including daily step counts, walking/running distances, and exercise targets
  • Sleep patterns and mindfulness duration data
  • Note: Health data integration requires explicit device authorization and is processed solely to visualize progress within the application interface.

Technical Logs & Telemetry

  • Device models, operating system versions, and native application runtime versions
  • Anonymized crash trace reporting and transaction telemetry
  • Secure push notification tokens

Artificial Intelligence Processing

User-submitted prompts, diagnostic strategies, and automated habit performance metrics required to generate personalized progress summaries and analytical insights.

2. How We Use Data

  • To power and maintain core native application functionality
  • To safely sync historical habit data securely across your connected devices via cloud databases
  • To deliver automated local or remote push notification routine reminders
  • To optimize large language model prompt strategies and analytical accuracy
  • To analyze application errors and protect architecture security

3. Third-Party Infrastructure

To deliver reliable services, we safely collaborate with vetted cloud infrastructure and subscription tooling providers: Google Firebase/Firestore (Data storage and authentication), RevenueCat (In-app purchase validation), Google Cloud/OpenAI APIs (AI-driven generation logic), and the Apple App Store / Google Play Console (App distribution and payment management).

4. Data Allocation & Protection

We do not lease or sell user personal or behavioral tracking data to third parties under any circumstances. Data shifts only occur when strictly obligated by compliance laws or to manage encrypted processing workflows with our explicit service providers.

5. Data Rights & Erasure

You maintain full authority over your data parameters. You hold the explicit right to review, update, export, or permanently erase your profile and habit logs at any moment directly through the account configurations inside the app.
